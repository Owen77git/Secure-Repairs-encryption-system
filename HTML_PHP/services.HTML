Our Services

At SecureRepair, we provide electronic repair solutions with a strong focus on data security, privacy, and trust. Every service we offer is backed by real-time monitoring and encrypted activity logging.

Phone Repairs

Professional repair of smartphones and tablets while keeping your personal data safe.

Laptop Repairs

Hardware and software fixes for laptops with full transparency via CCTV monitoring.

Data Protection

Encrypted repair logs ensure no technician can tamper with or hide their activities.

Real-time CCTV

Clients can monitor repairs live through our secure online portal at any time.

Client Notifications

Instant SMS/email alerts whenever sensitive files are accessed during repair.

Technician Authentication

Only authorized and vetted technicians can access tools and client devices.

Diagnostic Services

AI-assisted tools recommend the safest repair approach, reducing trial and error.

Integrity Reports

Clients receive a detailed post-repair report documenting all actions taken.

Quick Guide: How to Access Our Services

  1. Step 1: Visit our shop or register online.
  2. Step 2: Hand in your device and receive a repair agreement + access code.
  3. Step 3: Use our portal to monitor your device live during repair.
  4. Step 4: Receive instant notifications on key repair actions.
  5. Step 5: Collect your device and download the final integrity report.
