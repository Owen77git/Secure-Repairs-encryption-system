Goal of the System

The Secure Repair System was developed to stop the mishandling of client data in electronic repair shops. Too often, technicians have unauthorized access to photos, messages, and personal files during the repair process. This creates risks of extortion, blackmail, and loss of trust.

Our system introduces a secure monitoring framework built on four principles:

  • Real-time CCTV Monitoring -watch repairs live to ensure transparency.
  • Encrypted Repair Logs - every technician’s action is timestamped and tamper-proof.
  • Client Notifications - instant SMS/email alerts when sensitive data is accessed.
  • Technician Authentication - only authorized staff may use repair tools.

In short, the goal is to protect customer privacy, enforce accountability, and rebuild trust between clients and technicians.

Quick Manual Guide

  1. Register/Login: Access the system with your secure credentials.
  2. Check-In Device: Receive your repair agreement and unique access code.
  3. Monitor Repairs: Watch live CCTV feed of your device being handled.
  4. Receive Alerts: Get SMS/email notifications whenever sensitive files are accessed.
  5. Approve/Deny Access: Use your code to control technician permissions in real time.
  6. Download Report: At completion, receive an integrity report of all repair activities.

⚠️ Keep your access code safe — it is your personal control key.
